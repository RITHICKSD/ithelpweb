FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can Apex IT onboard my organisation? +
Most clients are fully onboarded within 30 days. For enterprise deployments, we provide a dedicated onboarding manager and a phased transition plan to ensure zero disruption.
Do you provide on-site support? +
Yes. Our Professional and Enterprise plans include on-site engineers available within 4 hours for P1 incidents. We have field technicians across all major cities.
What is your SLA for critical incidents? +
Priority 1 incidents receive a response within 15 minutes, 24/7/365. All SLAs are contractually binding with financial penalties for breach.
Can you manage our existing vendor contracts? +
Absolutely. Apex IT offers vendor management as part of our managed services, acting as your single point of contact for all technology suppliers.
Do you support hybrid cloud environments? +
Hybrid cloud is our speciality. We design, deploy and manage environments spanning on-premises, private and public cloud, with unified monitoring and governance.
What compliance frameworks do you cover? +
We support ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA, PCI DSS, NIST CSF, Cyber Essentials and more. Our compliance team provides ongoing guidance and audit preparation.
